How to Play Merge Conflict

Setup: Each player gets 10 white cards. One player is the Card Czar for the round.

Round Play: The Card Czar reads a black card out loud. Everyone else answers by playing a white card, face down.

Judging: The Card Czar picks the funniest combination. The player who played the winning white card gets a point.

Rotation: The role of Card Czar rotates to the next player each round.

Winning: First player to get the target number of points (or after set number of rounds) wins!

Developer Twist: All the humor is focused on programming, git conflicts, debugging, and developer life!
